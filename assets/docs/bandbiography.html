Biography

Neon Green were formed in North Liverpool in the Autumn of 2003. Initially playing covers, the band eventually started writing their own material, releasing their first album in 2005, 'Mersey Memories'. A UK tour shortly followed the release of their debut album, before the band headed back into the studio to record album number 2:'Anfield Dreams'. Neon Green comprises of: Jake Zywek-Lead Guitar & Lead Vocals. Reese Brown-Bass Guitar. Oliver Leigh-Second Guitar & Backing Vocals. Nathan Bellamy-Drums & Percussion.
